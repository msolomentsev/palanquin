Our Mission

Palanquin Power is an early-stage startup based in Golden, CO, developing novel power conversion architectures. Our unique approach has the ability to reduce losses during rack-level power conversion in data centers and other potential applications. We are a spin-out from the University of Texas at Austin and supported by the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL).

Our Impact

Data centers are enormous consumers of energy, and their demand for power is expected to grow rapidly over the next decade. Reducing losses in power conversion, and thus energy consumption, is a huge opportunity for this industry.
